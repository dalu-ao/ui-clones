Safari

The browser that was made for your Mac.

Safari is developed to run specifically on Apple made devices, with powerful privacy protections and industry - leading battery life.*

A browser that lets you do more online with up to 1.5 more hours of streaming time and 30 more minutes of browsing time on a single battery charge.*

Strong security protections in Safari help keep you safe. Password monitoring alerts you and helps you change your password if you’re involved in a data breach.

Safe and easy online shopping. Complete Apple Pay transactions with Touch ID or Face ID within Safari on all your Apple devices.

Safari is the world’s fastest browser with the ability to open even complex websites with amazing speed.**

Works seamlessly with Google Docs, Google Sheets and Google Slides so you can share and collaborate across all your files.

Intelligent Tracking Prevention identifies trackers and helps prevent them from profiling or following you across the web.

②
